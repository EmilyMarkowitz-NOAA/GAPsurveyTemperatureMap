NOAA Fisheries AFSC Groundfish Assessment Program conducted the Aleutian Islands bottom trawl survey. The near real-time ocean bottom temperatures depicted were collected June 10-August 13. On August 13, stations 162-16 (51.65°N, -177.89°W; >5.5–6°C) and 184-16 (51.66°N, -176.25°W; >5–5.5°C) were surveyed by the F/V Alaska Provider. No stations were surveyed by the F/V Ocean Explorer. Allocated stations that have not yet been sampled are shown as gray dots. Credit: NOAA Fisheries
