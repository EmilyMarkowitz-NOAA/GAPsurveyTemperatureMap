NOAA Fisheries AFSC Groundfish Assessment Program conducted the Aleutian Islands bottom trawl survey. The near real-time ocean bottom temperatures depicted were collected June 10-July 13. On July 13, a station 171-15 (51.6°N, -177.22°W; >4–4.5°C) was surveyed by the F/V Alaska Provider and stations 125-7 (51.27°N, 179.32°W; >4.5–5°C), 123-10 (51.38°N, 179.17°W; >5.5–6°C), 120-9 (51.35°N, 178.95°W; >4.5–5°C), 118-11 (51.45°N, 178.76°W; >4.5–5°C), and 118-11 (51.43°N, 178.8°W; >4.5–5°C) were surveyed by the F/V Ocean Explorer. Allocated stations that have not yet been sampled are shown as gray dots. Credit: NOAA Fisheries
