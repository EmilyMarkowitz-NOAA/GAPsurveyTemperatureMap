NOAA Fisheries AFSC Groundfish Assessment Program conducted the eastern Bering Sea and northern Bering Sea bottom trawl surveys. The near real-time ocean bottom temperatures depicted were collected May 29-August 20. This is the last day of the survey. On August 20, stations R-18 (60.67°N, -168.69°W; >8°C), R-01 (60.67°N, -168.01°W; >8°C), and R-02 (60.67°N, -167.32°W; >8°C) were surveyed by the F/V Alaska Knight. No stations were surveyed by the F/V Vesteraalen. Credit: NOAA Fisheries
