NOAA Fisheries AFSC Groundfish Assessment Program conducted the eastern Bering Sea and northern Bering Sea bottom trawl surveys. The near real-time ocean bottom temperatures depicted were collected May 29-July 13. On July 13, stations E-22 (56.33°N, -170.62°W; >4–5°C) and F-23 (56.67°N, -171.29°W; >3–4°C) were surveyed by the F/V Alaska Knight and stations M-23 (59°N, -171.77°W; >0–1°C), N-23 (59.33°N, -171.84°W; >-1–0°C), N-22 (59.33°N, -171.18°W; >-1–0°C), and O-22 (59.67°N, -171.25°W; >-1–0°C) were surveyed by the F/V Vesteraalen. There are 8 stations planned for July 14. Credit: NOAA Fisheries
