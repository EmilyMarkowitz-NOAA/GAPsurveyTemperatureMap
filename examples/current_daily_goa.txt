NOAA Fisheries AFSC Groundfish Assessment Program conducted the Gulf of Alaska bottom trawl survey. The near real-time ocean bottom temperatures depicted were collected May 23-August 14. On August 14, a station 447-60 (54.79°N, -133.07°W; >6–7°C) was surveyed by the F/V Alaska Provider. No stations were surveyed by the F/V Ocean Explorer. Allocated stations that have not yet been sampled are shown as gray dots. Credit: NOAA Fisheries
