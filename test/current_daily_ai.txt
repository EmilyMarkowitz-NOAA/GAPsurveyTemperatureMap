NOAA Fisheries AFSC Groundfish Assessment Program conducted the Aleutian Islands bottom trawl survey. The near real-time ocean bottom temperatures depicted were collected on June 10 2022. This is the first day of the survey. On June 10, stations 323-77 (54.38°N, -165.79°W; >4–4.5°C), 324-77 (54.36°N, -165.7°W; >4.5–5°C), and 326-76 (54.34°N, -165.55°W; >4.5–5°C) were surveyed by the F/V Ocean Explorer. No stations were surveyed by the F/V Alaska Provider. Allocated stations that have not yet been sampled are shown as gray dots. Credit: NOAA Fisheries
